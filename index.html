The Problem

The most structured thinkers in business.
The most broken career search.

Consultants are elite problem-solvers, yet when it comes to finding their next role, they're stuck in the same noisy, generic job boards as everyone else.

The Consultant Pain
"I'm on a project 60 hrs/week. When do I have time to job hunt?"
"I applied to 200 roles on LinkedIn. Got 3 responses. None relevant."
"I receive 30 job alerts every week. Most are irrelevant."
The Employer Pain
Companies can't find the consultants they actually want.
Job boards deliver hundreds of unqualified applicants.
The best consultant talent is passive. They never apply.
High noise-to-signal ratio on both sides. That's the gap we fill.
